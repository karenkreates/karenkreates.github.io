Who Am I?

Hey! My name is Karen Lee and I'm studying Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) at the University of Waterloo. I've always been in love with design. I was that kid who spent way too much time on the visual element of an assignment -- and I enjoy every second of it. I love learning new concepts and software in this ever-changing field.
